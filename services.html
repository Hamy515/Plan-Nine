Services

From spark to screen.

Three practices
i.

Development

It all begins with an idea. Our team has extensive experience developing projects from an early stage. Services include script editing, pitch deck and lookbook creation, initial budgeting and scheduling, casting assistance and creating financing plans.

ii.

Production

Here to serve. We jump on board to provide full production services and have experience across all stages of production, as well as an array of contacts, to ensure your project is delivered on budget and on schedule.

iii.

International Co-Production

We’re all about collaboration. Plan Nine Pictures is set up to co-produce internationally — leveraging our expertise and network, alongside the financial incentives and funding available in the UK.
