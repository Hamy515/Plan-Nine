A small group of operators.

Chris Hamilton

Producer · Co-Founder
Barcelona

Chris is a filmmaker and producer based in Barcelona. His debut feature film, A Million Days, premiered at FrightFest in 2023 and was released in 2024 by Signature Entertainment.

Chris has a background in development, having worked with executive producer Charlie Moretti, who co-financed productions such as BBC/BFI-backed Body of Water and FrightFest’s Concrete Plans.

His short films have been showcased at premier, Oscar-qualifying festivals including HollyShorts, SITGES, Aesthetica and Short Shorts Tokyo, as well as highly regarded international stages such as Manchester, Trieste S+F and FrightFest.

Charles Meunier

Producer · Co-Founder
London

Charles is a British-French producer. His debut feature, A Million Days, directed by Mitch Jenkins, is represented globally by Signature Entertainment and screened at FrightFest and the Trieste Science+Fiction Festival.

He has produced over 15 shorts across 100+ international festivals, including Oscar-longlisted Largo (starring Tamsin Greig) and the Icelandic co-production Whim Rules the Child. Charles is also an NFTS-trained Production Accountant, having served as 1st Assistant Production Accountant on HBO’s The Girl Before and Netflix’s I Came By and And Mrs.

Christophe Meslin

Head of Interactive Development
London

Christophe has over 26 years of experience in animation and VFX, beginning his career at Apple and Walt Disney Feature Animation. Now Framestore’s Global Head of Technical Resources, he manages procurement for VFX productions worldwide, supporting over 2,500 artists across four continents.

He has contributed to over 60 blockbusters, including Harry Potter, Avatar, Gravity, and the recent Marvel sagas. At Plan Nine Pictures, Christophe oversees animation and interactive project development, bringing his extensive network and expertise to the team.

Kelia Ideishi

Assistant Producer
London

Originally from Philadelphia, Kelia holds a BA in Art History from Temple University, where she received the Gundersheimer Award. She gained early experience at The Print Center Gallery and Ruby Bird Studios, honing her skills in art, photo, and film studio management and production.

Since 2018, Kelia has worked globally as a model for clients such as ELLE, Mulberry and Uniqlo, and has appeared in commercials for Apple, Bentley and Dyson. She is also developing a series of short films and scripts as a writer and director.

Fergus Browne

Development Consultant
London

Fergus holds an MA in Screenwriting from the University of the Arts London, graduating with distinction. Fergus has worked as a script editor on feature and TV projects, read for multiple competitions and companies, and interned at Celsius Entertainment as Sales and Acquisitions Assistant.

At Snapper Films, he worked on various short films as a Production Runner.
